My TON wallet

    Description

    This flow allows creating the first account in your wallet. This flow contains from several steps:

    1. Password creating
    1. Password checking
    1. Confirm your responsibility
    1. Save your seed phrase
    1. Tick the checkbox about that you have saved in security place
    1. Take from the giver or fill up the balance on this address
    1. Deploy the wallet
    1. Deployment process
    1. Success
    1. Your first account in the new wallet
