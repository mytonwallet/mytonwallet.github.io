    Restore a new wallet

    This flow allows restoring the first account in your wallet from keystore. This flow contains from several steps:

    1. Password creating
    1. Password checking
    1. Upload your keystore
    1. Confirm keystore
    1. Decrypt keystore by password. The hint can be useful.
    1. Confirm your password
    1. Review accounts and tick that you want to restore
    1. You will see which accounts are restored or already existed
    1. Done
    1. Your wallet is restored and ready for using
